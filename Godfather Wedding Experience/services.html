The Godfather Wedding Experience

A Personalised service just for you

We’ve never planned a wedding quite like yours, because yours is going to be totally unique, and we are excited about being a part of it.

However, we do have a wealth of experience, skill and knowledge which will take all of the stress out of planning and organising your perfect day.

Because your wedding day is so personal, so too is our service. We can do as much or as little as you’d like us to, from inspiration to co-ordination.

Planning

Turning your dream into a reality by organising the best suppliers and venues to suit both your vision and your budget.

Venue research & recommendations, Budget management, Sourcing suppliers & negotiating best possible rates, Etiquette and protocol advice, Civil and religious requirements, Creating a timeline and schedule.

Styling

From colour schemes to themes, we work with you to make sure your wedding day is both unique and personal, adding all of the finer details that will make it memorable and magical..

Style boards and inspirations, Colour palette and theme creation, Wedding stationery, Accessory/décor item sourcing and creation, Arranging rental of props including lighting, centrepieces and linens, On the day assistance with set-up and décor.

Co-ordination

Co-ordination Whether you want help co-ordinating your wedding throughout the entire planning process, in the month leading up to the wedding day, or just on the day itself – we can cover every element.

Phone and email support, Managing supplier payment schedules, Creating wedding day timeline and itinerary, Suggestions for guest accommodation & transport, Guest list RSVP tracking, Table plan creation & management, On the day co-ordination.

Above & Beyond

We like to go the extra mile to make sure you are both as happy and comfortable with every element of your wedding as possible.

Stag and Hen planning & co-ordination, Speech writing help and advice, Speech delivery coaching, Speech delivery coaching, Arranging professional babysitting & entertainment for children, Security and privacy service, Traditional Italian music and entertainment.
